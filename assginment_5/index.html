The Pleasure of Finding your Home

Purpose and Functionality

FindYourGspot is a campus-centric platform designed specifically for university students to rate and review various aspects of their campus experience. From colleges to dorm rooms and dining halls, students can find comprehensive ratings on cleanliness, noise levels, social life, and more.

Why It's Called "FindYourGspot"

The name is a playful, memorable take on helping students find their ideal spot on campus, whether it’s a dorm, a servery, or a study area. The "G" stands for Great, Good, and Genuine, representing the best places on campus that fit students’ lifestyles.

Slogan

"The Pleasure of Finding your Home" perfectly captures the joy of home-hunting, making the experience exciting and engaging.

Color Scheme

The FindYourGspot color scheme uses warm yellow and orange to create a welcoming and energetic feel. The soft cream adds balance and calm, reinforcing a positive and stress-free experience during the housing search.
